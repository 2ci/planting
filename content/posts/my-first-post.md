Welcome to the very first entry of my growth journal! On **April 11th**, I officially kicked off this season by seeding **six trays of basil**. I decided to start this blog specifically to document their journey from tiny black specks to lush, aromatic bushes.

Today is **April 15th**, and while it’s only been four days, the anticipation is building. The weather has been absolutely perfect lately, with temperatures hovering between **22°C and 28°C**. Basil is a true sun-lover, and this warm range is the "sweet spot" for germination. 

I’m checking the trays every morning for those first signs of green "elbows" breaking through the soil. With this consistent warmth, I’m hoping to see the first true leaves emerge quickly. There’s nothing quite like the smell of fresh basil, and I can't wait to see these trays transform. Stay tuned for the first sprouts!
